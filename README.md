## Previous Research Experience

### Advanced Materials Characterization

I have hands-on experience with a wide range of characterization techniques to investigate structural, chemical, thermal, and functional properties of materials:

- **X-ray Diffraction (XRD):** Phase identification, crystallinity analysis, and structural interpretation  
- **Raman Spectroscopy:** Phonon behavior, disorder analysis, and peak deconvolution  
- **X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS):** Surface chemistry and oxidation state analysis  
- **Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM/EDX):** Morphology and elemental composition  
- **BET Surface Area Analysis:** Surface area, pore size distribution, and porosity evaluation  
- **Zeta Potential Analysis:** Colloidal stability and interparticle interaction (DLVO-based interpretation)  
- **Thermal Analysis (TGA/DSC):** Thermal stability and kinetic behavior  
- **Magnetic Characterization (VSM/PPMS):** Magnetic response and structure–property correlation  

---

- Extracted physically meaningful parameters from experimental data for modeling and validation
